what we do

politics is broken and its tearing our world apart. until we change our political systems things will keep getting worse.

so what would different look like?

our role is to explore this question - by learning, working and making together - to build a shared imagination of a future that people can believe in.

we create

  • art, installations and experiences
  • meet-ups, workshops and events
  • cultural strategy and programmes
