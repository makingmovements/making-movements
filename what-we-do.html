what we do

making movements is a platform for artists and creatives of all kinds to imagine a new politics and bring it to life.

politics is broken and its tearing our world apart. until we change our political systems things will keep getting worse.

but change is all around us

we respond to the political alternatives emerging around the world, creatively, to build a shared imagination of a possible future.

we create

  • explorative sessions and creative workshops
  • meet-ups connections and opportunities
  • art, installations and experiences
  • cultural programmes and strategy
