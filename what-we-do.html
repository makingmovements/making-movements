what we do

making movements is a platform for artists of all kinds to explore political alternatives and bring them to life.

we create

  • art, installations and experiences
  • meet-ups, workshops and events
  • cultural strategy and programmes

to build a shared imagination of a future that people can believe in.

we believe

  • politics is broken and its tearing our world apart. until we change our political systems things will keep getting worse.
  • it doesn't have to be this way. different systems are emerging around the world showing what real democracy would look like.
  • politics is downstream of culture. to make change, we need to make the revolution creative, joyful and irresistible.
  • practice not theory. we learn by doing, making and experimenting.
