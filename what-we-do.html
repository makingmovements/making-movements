what we do

we believe

  • Our world is broken and politics has become the problem, not the solution.
  • We can do it differently. Innovations and alternatives are emerging.
  • We won't change our systems without public pressure. Culture can create this pressure.
  • Words are not enough. We learn by making and doing and feeling.

we create

  • art, events and experiences
  • explorative sessions and workshops
  • connections and opportunities
  • cultural programmes and strategy
  • a shared imagination of a different politics
