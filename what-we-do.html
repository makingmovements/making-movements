what we do

Making Movements is a collective of creatives of all kinds focusing on the question of our time: if politics is broken, how can we fix it?

we create:

  • explorative sessions and workshops
  • art, events and experiences
  • cultural programmes and strategy

we believe:

Our world is broken and politics has become the problem, not the solution.

Democratic innovations and alternatives are emerging around the world. But they are hidden in policy and academia.

We won't change our systems without public pressure. Political change requires cultural change.

We learn by making and doing, not just talking. everything we create builds our shared imagination.
