work with us

Making Movements brings together artists, creatives, communities, and organisations to explore what a new political culture could look like.

for artists

Join a growing community of people exploring political futures through creative practice.

We host field experiments, workshops, and collaborative projects where ideas are explored through making rather than debate alone.

Whether you're a musician, designer, writer, filmmaker, facilitator, performer, or simply curious, we'd love to hear from you.

for organisations

We design and facilitate participatory workshops, creative programmes, and cultural interventions.

  • participatory workshops and public events
  • creative facilitation and programme design
  • cultural strategy and advisory work
  • artist commissions and creative collaborations
  • bringing artists into conversations about participation, democracy, and social change
