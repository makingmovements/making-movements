work with us

for artists

a space to turn political frustration into creative action.

we host workshops in person and online, where artists, musicians, writers, designers, facilitators and curious people explore political futures through making.

these are spaces for experimentation, collaboration and constructive imagination.

whether you're a poet, electronic music producer, visual artist, organiser, researcher or simply interested in the future of politics, we'd love to hear from you.

for organisations

We bring artists, imagination and creative practice into conversations about participation, democracy and social change.

we can help with

  • artist commissions and collaborations
  • creative facilitation and participatory workshops
  • events and conferences
  • citizens' assemblies
  • cultural strategy
  • communications and storytelling

if you'd like to find out more about our work and how we can help, get in touch.
